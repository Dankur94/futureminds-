The OrbBot Workshop

Students build a self-balancing robot that stays upright on a ball — combining mechanical assembly, electronics, coding, and LQR control theory in one hands-on project.

Each student assembles their own OrbBot from our proprietary kit, programs it, tunes the controller, and takes it home. Designed and manufactured by KurthTechnik.

  • 4-hour guided workshop
  • OrbBot kit included for every student
  • IMU sensors, motors, microcontroller
  • LQR control — real engineering, not toy code
  • Students take their OrbBot home
  • Suitable for ages 14+ (secondary & university)
  • Groups of 6–30 students
  • On-site at your institution or at our studio
  • Taught in English or German
